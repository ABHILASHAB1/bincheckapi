Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about navigating cross-border payments, exchange rates, and financial validation.

How does the Live FX Dashboard get its data?

Our Live FX Dashboard pulls real-time institutional exchange rates directly from top global providers, updating every few seconds to ensure you see the true mid-market rate. We monitor spreads constantly so you can make informed decisions.

Is the BIN Checker tool completely free to use?

Yes, our Bank Identification Number (BIN) Checker is entirely free. You can look up the issuer bank, card type (credit/debit), and country of origin for any major credit card instantly without creating an account.

How accurate are the provider comparisons?

We track real-time spread data across all major remittance platforms. Our systems calculate the exact markup each provider adds on top of the mid-market rate, allowing you to see the true hidden costs before you commit to a transfer.

Do you facilitate actual money transfers?

Currently, RemitWise operates strictly as a financial intelligence and comparison hub. We provide you with the tools to find the best rates, but the actual transaction occurs on the provider's platform of your choice.
